I feel like a jigsaw puzzle missing a piece. And I'm not even sure what the picture should be.

He taught me a code. To survive. Keep your mind limber. You look…perfect. Cops, another community I'm not part of. Somehow, I doubt that. You have a good heart, Dexter.

Pretend. You pretend the feelings are there, for the world, for the people around you. Who knows? Maybe one day they will be. I'm really more an apartment person. Watching ice melt. This is fun. I'm partial to air conditioning.

Rorschach would say you have a hard time relating to others.

Oh I beg to differ, I think we have a lot to discuss. After all, you are a client. Like a sloth. I can do that. Makes me a … scientist. Tonight's the night. And it's going to happen again and again. It has to happen.
